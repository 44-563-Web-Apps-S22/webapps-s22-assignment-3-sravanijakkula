Biryanis and More

Biryanis and More is located at Benz Circle, Vijayawada, India. Ambience of this restaurant is beautiful and the environment is peaceful. It is near to city's most attractive point where students hangout most. It is a most happening place on weekends as it is in center of commerical hub.

Way To My Favourite Restaurant The nearest airport is Gannavaram Airport
  1. Take Right at the enterance of the airport and Walk for 15 minutes.
  2. Take Left and walk ahead for 3 minutes, you will reach a junction.
  3. At junction take left and continue walking for 10 minutes.
  4. After 10 minutes walk take 2nd right to reach Hyderabad Biryani at your right side 2nd buliding.
